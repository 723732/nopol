Context
=======

Software, something made by humans, is not perfect. Formally we call those imperfections "bugs". Informally something on the line of "wtf is happening?"

Solving bugs it's a manual and time consuming task. Wouldn't be great to have an automated way to solve (most) of them?

Introduction
============





Making it easy to solve may avoid "the blame game".


Semfix: An automated repair method based on symbolic execution, constraint solving and program synthesis. Semfix tries to fix a bug using symbolic execution, an SMT solver and program synthesis.
